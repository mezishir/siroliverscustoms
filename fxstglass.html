Faux Stained Glass

What is faux stained glass?


It is an acrylic paint that can be safely used on glass to create the look of stained glass without the matching price tag. It is easily removed from the glass and can be stored flat in Ziploc bags, if necessary, and reused.

Have you ever used the window clings during the holidays?


Imagine having something like that, but it is exactly the way you want it, unique to you and your style.


Also, you're not limited to holidays.

Faux stained glass is a great way to add color and beauty to glass, whether it is a storm door or a window. It can create abstract light, just as stained glass can, or offer privacy.


It is created using a water-based paint that is non-toxic. Each creation is permanent until removed. Be warned, however, that the paint does not like heat. Doors and windows that are susceptible to full sun will cause fading and melting of the artwork and are not advisable for faux stained glass.
